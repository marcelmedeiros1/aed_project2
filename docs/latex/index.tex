Welcome to the Air Travel Flight Management System! This system is designed to help users explore and plan their air travel efficiently. It leverages real-\/world data about airports, airlines, and flights to provide a comprehensive tool for making informed decisions.\hypertarget{index_autotoc_md2}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Dataset}{Dataset}}\label{index_autotoc_md2}
The system utilizes a dataset containing information about 3019 airports, 444 airlines, and 63832 flights. This dataset includes details such as airport codes, names, cities, countries, latitude, and longitude, among other information.\hypertarget{index_autotoc_md3}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Features}{Features}}\label{index_autotoc_md3}
